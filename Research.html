Research

Deb Roy Lab @ UConn Health

We study how the cytoskeletal dynamics mediate cell migration and adaptation to extracellular environments. Using advanced live-cell microscopy and synthetic biology approaches, we develop molecular tools to precisely control and visualize signaling pathways and cytoskeletal dynamics in real time to reveal how molecular signaling pathways produce complex cellular behaviors such as tissue development, cell migration and mechanosensing.
