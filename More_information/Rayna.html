Rayna Esch

Rayna is a medical student at the Frank H. Netter MD School of Medicine, completing her capstone project in the Deb Roy Lab. In collaboration with Connecticut Children's Medical Center and UConn Health, her work focuses on placental vascular biology in the context of fetal growth restriction and pre-eclampsia. In her free time, she enjoys hiking, playing soccer, spending time with family and friends, and volunteering to support individuals with intellectual disabilities.
